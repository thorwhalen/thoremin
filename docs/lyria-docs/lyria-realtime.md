Lyria RealTime unlocks the world of real-time music generation, allowing anyone to interactively create, control, and perform music in the moment.

### Capabilities

#### Moment-by-moment control

Lyria RealTime creates a continuous stream of music that never stops. You can steer, shape, and warp the music — all in real-time.

### Advanced controls

Blend multiple text prompts to mix genres, change instruments, and alter the mood of the music. You can also directly control the key, tempo, density, and brightness.

### Production-quality sound

Generate professional-grade 48kHz stereo audio, perfect for performance or as a source of inspiration for production.

### Musical surprises

Forge unlikely connections between existing sounds and genres to explore diverse new ideas.

Lyria RealTime creates a stream of music while responding to user controls. It ‘jams’ — like a musician in a band — playing new music based on its own output and user input. Users can shape and morph the music in real-time, mixing together styles, instruments, and musical attributes.
